About me

Eyo! My name is Isabel (she/her), but I usually go by LadyIsabel (or Lady of the Isles when I'm feeling pretentious) online. I'm a computer science student from Portugal in her 20's. Web and interface design are my favorite parts of working with computers. I wasn't very online when I was younger, but I grew up playing videogames, and over the years I started delving deeper and deeper into the web.

I'm a huge fantasy nut. Elder Scrolls, Dragon Age, and Guild Wars are some of my main brainrots. I also draw and write occasionally. I like to learn a little bit of pretty much anything and everything, though outside of my field of study, my favorite area is probably History.
