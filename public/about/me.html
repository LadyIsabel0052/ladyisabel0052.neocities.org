About me

Eyo! My name is Isabel (she/her), but I usually go by LadyIsabel (or Lady of the Isles when I'm feeling pretentious) online. I'm a 23-year-old trans woman from Portugal.

I have played videogames and messed around with computers for basically as long as I can remember, though I wasn't very online when I was younger. I was born a little too late to remember the web at a time before the social media juggernauts. I only really started having an online presence in my late teens, when I was introduced to Reddit and Discord.

From there, I've only delved deeper and deeper. I started joining community Discords of Youtube chanels I followed, and even ended up joining their moderation teams. I'm not very active on big community spaces anymore, but as I made friendships, I started to truly understand the value of the web.
